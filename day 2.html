About us

Chef Linguini la Costa is one of the best chef born in Milano, Italy. He studied fashion design but later became a chef by following his nonna's foot steps. Now, Linguini is an expert chef who has earned 3 Michellin stars for his outragious Italian inspired cuisine. He has mastered Italian cuisin at the age of 30 and now is reaching out to the world and let people know what food is all about. It's not about authenticity but rather about creativity. In June 2021, he opened his 10th restaurant in New York and named it Little Italy.

Little Italy is like the home of Linguini now. It's actually his $5 million mansion with the first floor being a 24/7 restaurant. In one interview with Forbes, he claimed: "Little Italy took 3 years to establish because of backlash against inauthentic Italian food. With the departure from Italy to the American market, it has become a big success and I am proud of my favorite child."
